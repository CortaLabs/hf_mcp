# Brotherhood thread template

Use this as a fill-in scaffold for Brotherhood-branded threads. Replace every bracketed placeholder before final output.

Required structure notes:
- Keep `[css=68]` around the full post body.
- Keep centered title + centered hero image at the top.
- Keep centered divider image blocks between major sections.
- Keep large centered section headers.
- Keep each section body specific, factual, and human-sounding.
- After drafting, run `$natural-writing` and complete its self-audit loop.

```bbcode
[css=68]
[center][size=7][b][TOPIC TITLE IN ALL CAPS OR STRONG TITLE CASE][/b][/size][/center]

[center][img][HERO_IMAGE_URL][/img][/center]

[center][size=2][b][ONE-LINE SUBTITLE OR HOOK][/b][/size][/center]

[center][img][DIVIDER_IMAGE_URL][/img][/center]

[center][size=5][b]1) [SECTION HEADER][/b][/size][/center]

[PARAGRAPH 1 WITH SPECIFIC CONTEXT: WHO/WHAT/WHEN. INCLUDE AT LEAST ONE VERIFIED FACT OR DATE.]

[PARAGRAPH 2 WITH A CONCRETE EXAMPLE OR COMPARISON. ADD A REAL OPINION OR TAKE, NOT GENERIC FILLER.]

[PARAGRAPH 3 WITH A PRACTICAL IMPLICATION FOR THE READER. IF UNSURE, MARK CLAIMS AS [VERIFY].]

[center][img][DIVIDER_IMAGE_URL][/img][/center]

[center][size=5][b]2) [SECTION HEADER][/b][/size][/center]

[PARAGRAPH 1: EXPLAIN THE CORE POINT IN PLAIN LANGUAGE.]

[PARAGRAPH 2: GIVE ONE NAMED EXAMPLE, STAT, OR CASE.]

[PARAGRAPH 3: ADD LIMITS OR TRADEOFFS. STATE WHAT IS STILL UNKNOWN WITH [VERIFY] TAGS.]

[center][img][DIVIDER_IMAGE_URL][/img][/center]

[center][size=5][b]3) [SECTION HEADER][/b][/size][/center]

[PARAGRAPH 1: WHAT CHANGES NEXT OR WHAT TO WATCH.]

[PARAGRAPH 2: READER TAKEAWAY WITH CLEAR ACTION OR QUESTION.]

[center][size=4][b][CLOSING LINE OR CALL TO ACTION][/b][/size][/center]
[/css]
```

## Natural-writing gate (required)

Before handoff, confirm all checks:
- I ran `$natural-writing` on the draft.
- I removed obvious AI wording patterns and filler phrases.
- I kept sentence rhythm varied and included specific details.
- I fact-checked claims or marked unresolved points as `[VERIFY]`.
- I completed the self-audit question: "What makes this obviously AI generated?" and revised until no obvious tells remained.
